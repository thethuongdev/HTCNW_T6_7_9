One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquaited with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the home less, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include Them Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
